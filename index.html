About Me

I am a MPhil student in HKU AIoT Lab supervised by Prof. Wu Chenshu and Prof. Wu Chuan. Before that, I got my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science from the University of Hong Kong with First Class Honours. I also obtain several extensive industrial experience in NVIDIA, miHoYo, etc.

My current research interest spans across GPU-Related Operating and Networking System, especially the intra-gpu-kernel level performance engineering. Previously I was also interested in mobile computing, human-computer interaction, web development, computer vision, etc.
